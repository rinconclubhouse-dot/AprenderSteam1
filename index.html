A table organizes information into a grid. It has rows (going side to side) and columns (going top to bottom). It is perfect for info cards and data!

What is a table in Word?
Parts of a table:
Feature Lion Dolphin
Type Mammal Mammal

←→ Row= one horizontal row

💡 In Word, you insert a table like this: click the Insert tab → click Table → choose how many rows and columns you need.

A card without an image is not complete! In Word you can insert photos from the internet or from your computer. Let me show you how!
CONCEPT · STEP 5
How to insert an image in Word
1
Click where you want the image
Place your cursor at the spot in the document where your photo will go.
2
Go to the Insert tab
It is in the top menu bar of Word, right next to "Home".
3
Click Pictures
Choose "Online Pictures" to search the internet, or "This Device" to use one you already have.
4
Search and select your image
Type your animal's name, choose a nice photo, and click Insert.
5
Resize it
Drag the corners of the image to make it bigger or smaller. Always use the corners so it does not get distorted!
💡 Tip: To make text wrap around the image, right-click on it → Wrap Text → Square.
